From The Creator

Dom Web has been a personal project of mine for a long time. Being a one-person crew, it has been hard to achieve what I have today. I love making aesthetically pleasing websites and hope to bring that joy to many more people. This will help small businesses gain a presence in a growing online world. The shopping page is just a little side project to experiment with how online shopping works. :) I hope to make that an official website soon!
